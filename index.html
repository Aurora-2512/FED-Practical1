MILK BREAD PUDDING

Ingredients

  • 200 mL of Milk
  • one Egg
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • a few amount of salt
  • bread
  • Caramel
    		•

    Choice

    1. Milk
      1. Soy Milk
      2. Low fat
    2. Sugar
      1. Equal
      2. Brown sugar

    Recipe

    First prepare an egg and beats until yolks and its white are uniformly mixed.
    Put 2 tbsp of sugar and a few salt into warm milk and stir.
    Place the stir egg and milk together into the bowl which will be put into the oven.
    Cut the bread into small pieces and add to the bowl.
    Then, bake in the oven for 20 min and enjoy your delicious dessert ever!!!
